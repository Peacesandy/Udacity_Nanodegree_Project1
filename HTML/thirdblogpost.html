Skin elasticity (a.k.a. the amount of bounce-back your skin has when pinched) is a key component of what keeps skin looking lifted and firm. As we age, our skin starts to lose elasticity, thanks to a decline in collagen and elastin production. Treating this can be tricky, but ingredients like growth factors play a pivotal role in maintaining firmness and elasticity in your skin. Daily use of topical skin care products containing growth factors are known to help reduce the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles and improve skin tone and texture.
